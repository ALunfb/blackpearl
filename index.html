What is a CS2 Black Pearl Doppler?
A Black Pearl Doppler is one of the rarest knife finishes in Counter-Strike 2 (CS2). It is a special phase of the Doppler skin pattern that features a dark, iridescent color palette mixing deep purples, greens, and blues. Black Pearls are significantly rarer and more expensive than standard Doppler phases (P1–P4), Ruby, or Sapphire variants.
How much is a Black Pearl knife worth in CS2?
Black Pearl knife prices vary widely depending on the knife type, wear condition, and float value. Prices can range from around $400 for less popular knives like the Navaja or Gut Knife to over $10,000 for a Factory New Karambit or Butterfly Knife Black Pearl. Check blackpearl.gg for live, up-to-date pricing from CSFloat listings.
How can I tell if a Doppler knife is a Black Pearl?
Black Pearls are identified by their paint index: 417 for most knives, or 617 for the Butterfly Knife and Shadow Daggers. In-game, they have a distinctive dark, oil-slick appearance with deep purple and green hues, unlike the brighter pinks and blues of standard Doppler phases. You can verify a knife's phase by inspecting it on CSFloat or using blackpearl.gg.
Which CS2 knives can have the Black Pearl finish?
There are 18 knife types that can drop with the Black Pearl Doppler finish in CS2: Karambit, Butterfly Knife, M9 Bayonet, Bayonet, Flip Knife, Gut Knife, Huntsman Knife, Bowie Knife, Falchion Knife, Stiletto Knife, Talon Knife, Skeleton Knife, Navaja Knife, Ursus Knife, Shadow Daggers, Paracord Knife, Survival Knife, and Nomad Knife.
What does float value mean for a Black Pearl knife?
Float value is a number between 0 and 1 that determines the wear condition of a CS2 skin. For Black Pearl knives: Factory New (FN) is 0.00–0.07, Minimal Wear (MW) is 0.07–0.15, Field-Tested (FT) is 0.15–0.38, Well-Worn (WW) is 0.38–0.45, and Battle-Scarred (BS) is 0.45–1.00. Lower float values mean less visible wear and typically command higher prices.
Where can I buy a CS2 Black Pearl knife?
Black Pearl knives can be purchased on third-party CS2 marketplaces like CSFloat. blackpearl.gg aggregates live CSFloat listings for all 18 Black Pearl knife types, showing current prices, float values, and direct links to buy. You can also inspect each knife in 3D before purchasing.
How rare is a Black Pearl Doppler drop in CS2?
Black Pearl is the rarest Doppler phase in CS2. When a Doppler knife drops, there is roughly a 1-in-250 chance it will be a Black Pearl, making it far rarer than standard phases (P1–P4) and even rarer than Ruby or Sapphire variants. This extreme rarity is what drives their premium pricing.
