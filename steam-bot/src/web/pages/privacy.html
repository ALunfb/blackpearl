Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how blackpearl.gg ("we", "us", or "the site") handles information when you visit our website. Our goal is to be transparent and minimize data collection.

Information We Do Not Collect

The main site (blackpearl.gg) does not require registration, does not collect accounts, does not track you across the web, and does not use third-party analytics or ad networks.

Automatically Collected Information

Like most websites, our hosting provider automatically logs basic request data:

  • IP address (for DDoS protection and bandwidth management)
  • HTTP headers (browser type, referrer)
  • Request path and timestamp

These logs are retained for a short period and used only for operational purposes. We do not sell or share this data.

Third-Party Services

Our site loads resources from the following third parties:

  • Google Fonts — to render site typography. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • CSFloat (csfloat.com) — displayed listing links and seller avatars. See CSFloat's Privacy Policy.
  • Steam CDN — knife preview images. See Steam's Privacy Policy.

Cookies

The main site does not use cookies.

The Steam Alerts dashboard at bot.blackpearl.gg uses a single session cookie (bp_session) to keep you logged in after signing in with Steam. This cookie is HTTP-only, secure (HTTPS), and automatically expires after 7 days. No tracking cookies are used.

Steam Alerts Bot — Data We Store

If you sign into the Steam Alerts dashboard, we store:

  • Your public Steam ID, display name, and avatar URL (fetched from the Steam Web API)
  • Your notification subscriptions (knife filters, price ranges, etc.)
  • A log of notifications sent to you for debugging and rate-limiting

We do not store your Steam password, email, or any payment information. Steam OpenID handles authentication.

Data Deletion

You may permanently delete all your data from the Steam Alerts dashboard at any time via the "Delete All My Data" button. This removes your account, subscriptions, and notification history from our systems.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
